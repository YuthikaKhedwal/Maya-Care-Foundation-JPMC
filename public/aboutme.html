About us

Maya CARE was conceived as a dream to assist senior citizens in activities that would offer them convenience, independence and happiness while continuing to live in their own homes. The dream is now a movement propelled by a growing network of volunteers across cities and supporters across the world. Maya CARE services are offered FREE of charge.
